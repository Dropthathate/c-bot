03

Voice & Audio Processing California Disclosure

SomaSyncAI's core functionality includes real-time voice input processing. This section provides full disclosure of how audio data is handled.

How voice data is used:

  • Voice input is processed in real-time to convert spoken clinical observations into structured documentation
  • Audio may be temporarily processed by AI systems to generate SOAP notes and treatment summaries
  • Voice data is used to improve transcription accuracy and AI model performance, in de-identified form only

Audio retention:

  • SomaSyncAI does not intentionally store raw audio recordings beyond what is necessary for real-time processing
  • Processed text output (generated notes) is retained as part of your account records
  • You may request deletion of your voice-derived data at any time
Practitioner Responsibility: Clinicians using SomaSyncAI are responsible for ensuring they have obtained appropriate informed consent from patients before capturing or documenting patient information during sessions. SomaSyncAI is a tool for the practitioner's documentation — not a patient-facing application.

California Privacy Rights CCPA / CPRA

California residents have the following rights under the CCPA and CPRA. These rights are available to you at no charge and may be exercised up to twice per 12-month period:

📋
Right to Know

Request disclosure of the categories and specific pieces of personal information we have collected about you, and how it has been used or shared.

🗑
Right to Delete

Request deletion of personal information we have collected, subject to certain legal exceptions.

✏️
Right to Correct

Request correction of inaccurate personal information we maintain about you.

📦
Right to Portability

Receive a copy of your personal information in a portable, machine-readable format.

🚫
Right to Opt-Out

Opt out of the sale or sharing of personal information. We do not sell data, but you may opt out of sharing for cross-context advertising purposes.

🔒

To submit a request, email privacy@somasyncai.com with the subject line "California Privacy Request." We will verify your identity and respond within 45 days (extendable by an additional 45 days with notice).

Cookies & Tracking CalOPPA

Under CalOPPA, we disclose how we respond to Do Not Track signals and our cookie practices.

  • Essential Cookies: Required for the platform to function; cannot be disabled
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand usage patterns (opt-out available)
  • Preference Cookies: Remember your settings and personalisation choices

Do Not Track: SomaSyncAI currently does not respond to browser-level Do Not Track (DNT) signals, as there is no uniform industry standard for DNT compliance. You may manage tracking preferences via your account settings or browser controls.
