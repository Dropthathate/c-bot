What Our AI Does

SomaSyncAI uses artificial intelligence to assist licensed practitioners with real-time clinical documentation. Our AI is a documentation support tool — nothing more.

The AI Can
  • Convert spoken clinical observations into structured documentation
  • Generate SOAP note drafts from voice input
  • Identify patterns in session data you provide
  • Suggest documentation items based on observed clinical language
  • Build a historical record of sessions within your account
  • Provide general wellness and documentation guidance
The AI Cannot
  • Diagnose medical or psychological conditions
  • Prescribe or recommend medications or treatments
  • Replace a licensed physician, therapist, or clinician
  • Guarantee clinical accuracy of generated documentation
  • Access real-time medical literature or clinical databases
  • Make autonomous clinical decisions of any kind

AI Limitations & Accuracy AI Transparency

SomaSyncAI discloses the following known limitations of our AI systems in the interest of transparency:

  • Hallucination: AI models can generate plausible-sounding but factually incorrect information. This is an inherent limitation of current large language model technology. All AI outputs must be critically reviewed.
  • Incomplete Context: Our AI only processes the information you provide. It has no access to a patient's complete medical history, diagnostic records, or external clinical data.
  • Training Data Limitations: AI outputs reflect patterns in training data, which may not represent the most current clinical guidelines or emerging research.
  • Bias: AI systems may reflect biases present in their training data. We actively work to identify and reduce these, but cannot guarantee bias-free outputs.
  • Speciality Limitations: Our AI is designed for general clinical documentation support. It may be less accurate in highly specialised clinical contexts.
  • Language & Dialect: AI voice processing accuracy may vary based on accent, speech patterns, background noise, or clinical terminology usage.
We are committed to ongoing improvement of our AI systems. If you encounter an inaccurate, inappropriate, or potentially harmful AI output, please report it immediately to [email protected].

Voice & Audio AI California Disclosure

SomaSyncAI's primary AI feature involves real-time voice input processing. We disclose the following about how this works:

  • Voice input is processed in real-time by AI systems to generate structured documentation
  • Audio is converted to text; raw audio recordings are not retained beyond what is required for real-time processing
  • AI transcription accuracy depends on audio quality, speech clarity, and clinical terminology used
  • Voice-derived text data may be used in de-identified form to improve transcription accuracy
Patient Consent Reminder: California law and professional ethics require practitioners to obtain informed consent from patients before recording, documenting, or otherwise processing patient information during clinical sessions. The use of SomaSyncAI during a patient session is the practitioner's responsibility to disclose to the patient as appropriate.
