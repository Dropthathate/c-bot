🏢 General Questions

Learn about SomaSync AI and our mission

What is SomaSync AI?

+

SomaSync AI is the first AI-powered, hands-free clinical assistant designed specifically for manual therapy practitioners. Our platform combines Natural Language Processing, real-time speech recognition, and anatomical intelligence to revolutionize clinical documentation.

The system listens to your verbal observations during postural assessments and treatment sessions, automatically identifies anatomical structures and deviations, and generates structured SOAP notes in real-time—all while keeping your hands free to focus on your clients.

Who is SomaSync AI for?

+

SomaSync AI is designed for manual therapy practitioners including:

  • Licensed Massage Therapists (LMTs)
  • Physical Therapists (PTs)
  • Chiropractors
  • Myofascial Release Practitioners
  • Manual Osteopaths
  • Bodywork Students and Educators

Any manual therapy professional who performs postural assessments and needs to document findings can benefit from SomaSync AI.

What development stage is SomaSync AI currently in?

+

SomaSync AI is currently in Phase I Development as of Q1 2026. We are:

  • Developing and validating our core AI model for anatomical recognition
  • Building the minimum viable product (MVP) with mobile integration
  • Preparing for pilot testing with 10-15 licensed therapists

We expect to begin Phase I pilot testing in Q3 2026, with a broader Phase II beta program in Q4 2026 involving 50+ practitioners. Full commercial launch is planned for 2027.

Is SomaSync AI available now?

+

Not yet. SomaSync AI is under active development and not available for public use. We are currently accepting applications for our Phase I pilot program, which will begin in Q3 2026.

What problem does SomaSync AI solve?

+

Manual therapists face several critical documentation challenges:

  • Lost Data: Relying on memory leads to incomplete records
  • Time Burden: 10-15 minutes per session spent on documentation
  • Unstandardized Assessment: No consistent recording method
  • Compliance Risk: Incomplete documentation affects billing and legal defensibility
  • Workflow Disruption: Breaking away from hands-on work interrupts therapeutic flow

SomaSync AI solves these problems by enabling hands-free, AI-powered documentation that captures observations in real-time without disrupting the session.

⚡ Product Features

Understanding what SomaSync AI can do

How does the hands-free operation work?

+

SomaSync AI uses your smartphone's microphone or a Bluetooth earbud to capture your verbal observations during sessions. Simply speak naturally as you work:

"Mark restricted right upper trapezius"
"Anterior pelvic tilt"
"Elevated left scapula"

The AI engine processes your speech in real-time, identifies anatomical structures, maps deviations, and time-stamps all observations. You never need to stop what you're doing to take notes.

What is the SomaSphere?

+

The SomaSphere is our collaborative insight engine that transforms individual assessments into community-powered wisdom. After identifying a deviation like "anterior pelvic tilt," you can tap the SomaSphere icon to access:

  • Interactive 3D anatomical models
  • Heat maps showing prevalence of similar patterns across demographics
  • Population-level musculoskeletal health trends
  • Context for understanding if your client's issue is part of a broader pattern

This gives you unprecedented clinical context by showing how office workers, athletes, or specific age groups present with similar patterns.

What are SyncLearn Modules?

+

SyncLearn provides just-in-time education during your sessions. When SomaSync suggests a pattern like "Upper Crossed Syndrome," a Learn icon appears. Tapping it launches a 30-90 second module featuring:

  • Tutorial videos on specific palpation techniques
  • Interactive anatomical diagrams
  • Visual guides to involved muscle groups
  • Brief explanations of associated TCM patterns

This turns every session into a learning opportunity, reinforcing knowledge and introducing new techniques directly within your workflow.

What is the Client Portal?

+

The Client Portal is a patient-facing application that creates a complete care ecosystem. Clients can:

  • Track their recovery progress with visualizations
  • Log symptoms before and after appointments
  • Report how they're feeling post-session
  • View their treatment history and progress

As a practitioner, you benefit from:

  • Cross-referencing your observations with client-reported symptoms
  • Receiving alerts when clients report concerning symptoms
  • AI-suggested follow-ups or medical referrals when appropriate
  • Enhanced client engagement and accountability

How accurate is the AI recognition?

+

In controlled testing, our AI model achieves ≥80% accuracy in identifying anatomical tissues, deviations, and laterality (left/right). Our Phase I goal is to maintain this accuracy in real-world clinical settings.

The system is trained specifically on clinical language used by manual therapists and continues to improve through use. You can always review and edit transcriptions in real-time, and the system learns from corrections.

What anatomical structures does SomaSync AI recognize?

+

SomaSync AI's Knowledge Engine understands clinical language related to:

  • Muscles: 100+ muscles and muscle groups
  • Joints: Major articulations throughout the body
  • Soft Tissue: Tendons, ligaments, and fascia
  • Nerves: Major nerve pathways
  • Deviations: Postural deviations and movement patterns
  • Patterns: Upper Crossed Syndrome, Lower Crossed Syndrome, and other compensation patterns

Can I edit the AI-generated notes?

+

Yes, absolutely. SomaSync generates draft notes that you can review, edit, and approve before finalizing. You maintain complete control over your clinical documentation.

You can see the transcription in real-time during your session and make corrections as needed. The system learns from these corrections to improve accuracy over time.

🔧 Technical Questions

Equipment, compatibility, and technical requirements

What equipment do I need?

+

SomaSync AI is designed to work with equipment you likely already have:

  • Smartphone: iPhone (iOS) or Android device
  • Audio Input: Built-in microphone OR Bluetooth earbud/headset
  • Internet Connection: For cloud processing and sync

No special equipment is required. For optimal hands-free operation, we recommend any Bluetooth earbud. We will also offer a recommended in-ear device optimized for clinical environments.

What phones and operating systems are supported?

+

SomaSync AI will support:

  • iOS: iPhone 8 and newer, iOS 14 or later
  • Android: Android 9.0 (Pie) or newer

The app is being developed as a native mobile application for both platforms to ensure optimal performance and integration with device features.

Do I need internet connection to use SomaSync AI?

+

For the MVP (Phase I), yes, an internet connection is required for:

  • Real-time AI processing and analysis
  • Cloud synchronization across devices
  • Access to SomaSphere and SyncLearn content

Future versions will include an offline mode that captures and stores data locally, then syncs when connectivity is restored.

Will SomaSync integrate with my existing EHR system?

+

Phase I focuses on copy-to-clipboard functionality, allowing you to easily paste generated notes into any system you currently use.

Phase II will include direct integrations with major EHR and practice management systems including:

  • Massage Book
  • Jane App
  • SimplePractice
  • Cliniko
  • Other popular platforms based on user demand

What languages does SomaSync AI support?

+

Phase I supports English only. We are focusing on perfecting the English clinical language model before expanding.

Additional languages (Spanish, Mandarin, Portuguese, etc.) will be added in future phases based on user demand and market research.

What happens if the AI doesn't understand what I say?

+

If SomaSync doesn't recognize or misunderstands something, you can:

  • View the real-time transcription and see what was captured
  • Repeat or rephrase your observation
  • Manually edit the transcription
  • Add typed notes for anything not captured by voice

The system learns from corrections, so accuracy improves over time with use.

🔒 Privacy & Security

Your data safety is our priority

Is SomaSync AI HIPAA compliant?

+

HIPAA compliance is under active development. We are building SomaSync AI with HIPAA compliance as a core requirement from day one. Our architecture includes:

  • End-to-end encryption for all data
  • Secure cloud storage on Microsoft Azure
  • Role-based access controls
  • Comprehensive audit trails
  • Business Associate Agreements (BAAs)

Full HIPAA compliance will be verified and certified before commercial launch.

How is my client data protected?

+

We implement multiple layers of security:

  • Encryption: All data encrypted in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest (AES-256)
  • Access Control: Multi-factor authentication and role-based permissions
  • Cloud Security: Hosted on Microsoft Azure with enterprise-grade security
  • Regular Audits: Third-party security assessments and penetration testing
  • Backup & Recovery: Automated backups with disaster recovery protocols

Who has access to my data?

+

You maintain complete control. Your client data is:

  • Owned entirely by you
  • Accessible only to you and authorized users you designate
  • Never sold or shared with third parties
  • Used only for providing the service you subscribed to

SomaSphere population data is anonymized and aggregated, with no personally identifiable information included.

What happens to my data if I cancel?

+

Upon cancellation:

  • Your data remains accessible for 90 days
  • You can export all documentation in standard formats
  • After 90 days, data is permanently deleted from our systems
  • You can request immediate deletion at any time

We provide easy export tools to ensure you never lose access to your clinical records.

Does SomaSync AI record audio from sessions?

+

No permanent audio recordings are made. Audio is:

  • Processed in real-time for speech-to-text conversion
  • Immediately discarded after transcription
  • Never stored on our servers
  • Never transmitted beyond the transcription service

Only the text transcription and AI-generated insights are saved to your clinical records.
