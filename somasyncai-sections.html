Data & Security

YOUR DATA.
YOUR RESPONSIBILITY.
OUR ARCHITECTURE.

SomaSyncAI is a clinical operating system — a tool you control. Here's exactly how data flows and what that means for your practice.

⚖️

HIPAA: Your Practice, Your Obligation

SomaSyncAI is a documentation and guidance tool — similar to a word processor or EHR system. As with any clinical software, you as the practitioner are the HIPAA Covered Entity. You control what patient information enters the system and how it is stored, shared, and protected. We are committed to building infrastructure that supports your compliance obligations as we grow.

🔒

You Control Your Data

Your session notes, client records, and voice data belong to you — not us. SomaSyncAI does not sell, license, or share your clinical data with third parties. Period.

🛡️

Encrypted in Transit & at Rest

All data transmitted between your device and our servers is encrypted via TLS. Stored session data uses industry-standard encryption at rest. Your notes don't travel in the clear.

🎙️

Voice Processing Transparency

Voice input is processed in real time to generate documentation. Audio is not permanently stored after note generation is complete. You document — we don't archive your recordings.

🚫

No Selling. No Training on Your Data.

Your clinical sessions are never used to train AI models without explicit opt-in consent. We do not monetize your patient data. Full stop.

A note for practitioners evaluating compliance: We recommend reviewing our Privacy Policy and AI Disclaimer with your compliance advisor. As SomaSyncAI grows toward enterprise tiers, BAA (Business Associate Agreement) support is on our roadmap. Questions? hello@somasyncai.com
