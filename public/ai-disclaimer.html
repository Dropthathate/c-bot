01

What SomaSyncAI Is

SomaSyncAI is a software platform that uses artificial intelligence to assist licensed manual therapy practitioners with:

  • Real-time clinical documentation support during patient sessions
  • Automated generation of SOAP note drafts from voice input
  • Suggestions for ICD-10 diagnostic codes based on documented clinical findings
  • In-ear guidance and clinical reasoning prompts during assessments
  • Treatment memory and longitudinal pattern recognition across sessions

SomaSyncAI is designed to reduce administrative burden and support clinical efficiency. It is a practitioner tool, not a clinical decision-making authority.

02

What SomaSyncAI Is Not

SomaSyncAI is not:

  • A licensed medical device under FDA or equivalent regulatory authority
  • A diagnostic tool — it does not diagnose conditions, diseases, or injuries
  • A substitute for clinical training, professional licensure, or practitioner judgment
  • A prescribing system — it does not recommend medications or prescriptions
  • A replacement for specialist consultation when clinically indicated
  • A guarantee of clinical accuracy, completeness, or legal compliance of any generated documentation
  • A substitute for a licensed Electronic Health Record (EHR) system

Think of AALIYAH as an exceptionally knowledgeable clinical colleague standing beside you — offering insights, prompting your thinking, and capturing your documentation. She doesn't hold the license. You do.

03

Practitioner Responsibility

By using SomaSyncAI, you acknowledge and agree that:

  • You are the clinician of record. All clinical decisions, treatment plans, and documentation are your professional responsibility.
  • AI outputs require review. Every SOAP note, ICD-10 suggestion, contraindication flag, and clinical recommendation generated by SomaSyncAI must be reviewed and verified by you before being used, signed, billed against, or shared.
  • You maintain your license obligations. Your professional licensing board's standards of practice govern your work. SomaSyncAI does not modify, replace, or create exemptions from those obligations.
  • Contraindication flags are prompts, not diagnoses. When AALIYAH flags a potential contraindication, this is a prompt for your clinical judgment — not a definitive clinical finding.
  • Documentation accuracy is your responsibility. Generated SOAP notes are drafts. You are responsible for ensuring all submitted documentation is accurate, complete, and compliant with payer and regulatory requirements.
  • ICD-10 codes require verification. AI-suggested diagnostic codes are starting points. You or your billing team are responsible for final code selection and compliance with payer policies.
04

Limitations of AI

Artificial intelligence, including the systems powering SomaSyncAI, has inherent limitations that every user should understand:

  • AI can be wrong. AI systems generate outputs based on patterns in training data. They can produce inaccurate, incomplete, or contextually inappropriate outputs.
  • AI cannot examine patients. SomaSyncAI relies on your verbal observations. It cannot palpate tissue, observe subtle visual cues, or perform physical assessments.
  • AI does not know your patient. While treatment memory improves over time, AI does not have the full clinical relationship, intuition, or contextual understanding that a practitioner develops with a patient.
  • AI reflects its training data. Our models may not account for rare presentations, atypical populations, or emerging clinical research not present in training data.
  • AI may miss context. Voice recognition and clinical language processing may misinterpret spoken words, especially in noisy environments, with accented speech, or when technical terminology is used in unusual ways.
