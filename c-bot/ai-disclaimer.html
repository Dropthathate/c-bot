What Our AI Does

SomaSyncAI's artificial intelligence features are designed to enhance your personal wellness journey by providing contextual information, pattern recognition, and motivational support based on the information you share with us.

The AI can
  • Offer general wellness information and educational content
  • Identify patterns in the data you provide
  • Suggest general lifestyle habits aligned with your stated goals
  • Provide motivational prompts and accountability reminders
  • Help you track trends in your self-reported wellbeing
The AI cannot
  • Diagnose medical or psychological conditions
  • Prescribe or recommend medications or clinical treatments
  • Replace a licensed physician, therapist, or dietitian
  • Guarantee outcomes or health improvements
  • Access real-time medical records or clinical data
02

What Our AI Does Not Do

To be unambiguous about the boundaries of our AI features, the following are explicitly outside the scope of SomaSyncAI:

  • Clinical assessment or psychological evaluation of any kind
  • Interpretation of medical test results, scans, or lab reports
  • Provision of emergency health guidance or crisis intervention
  • Nutritional prescriptions for medical conditions (e.g. diabetes management)
  • Fitness programming for individuals with diagnosed injuries or chronic conditions without professional supervision
  • Predictions or guarantees regarding health outcomes
03

Not Medical Advice

Critical Disclaimer All content generated by SomaSyncAI — including text, recommendations, summaries, and wellness plans — is for general informational and motivational purposes only. It does not constitute medical advice and should not be relied upon as such.

Before making any changes to your diet, exercise routine, medication, or health regimen, you should always consult with a qualified healthcare provider who can evaluate your individual circumstances.

The information provided by our AI does not establish a patient-provider relationship, and no professional duty of care arises from your use of SomaSyncAI.

04

AI Limitations

All AI systems — including ours — have inherent limitations that users should be aware of:

  • Accuracy: AI-generated content may be incomplete, outdated, or contextually incorrect. It reflects patterns in training data, not verified clinical expertise.
  • Hallucination: AI models can occasionally generate plausible-sounding but factually incorrect information. Always cross-reference health claims with reliable sources.
  • Personalisation limits: Insights are based on information you provide. Incomplete or inaccurate input will affect the quality of output.
  • Bias: AI systems may reflect biases present in training data. We continuously work to identify and mitigate these, but no AI is entirely free of bias.
  • No real-time awareness: Our AI does not have access to live medical literature, real-time clinical guidelines, or your medical history outside what you share.
We are committed to continuous improvement. If you notice an AI output that appears inaccurate or harmful, please report it to feedback@somasyncai.com.
05

Mental Health

SomaSyncAI may offer general mindfulness, stress-management, and emotional wellbeing features. These are supportive tools only and are not a substitute for professional mental health care.

If You Are in Crisis If you are experiencing a mental health crisis, suicidal thoughts, or are in immediate danger, please contact emergency services or a crisis helpline in your country immediately. SomaSyncAI is not equipped to provide crisis intervention.

Our AI is designed to disengage from and redirect conversations that involve expressions of self-harm, suicidal ideation, or acute psychological distress, and to prompt users toward appropriate professional resources.

  • Using AI chat features as a replacement for therapy or psychiatric care
  • Sharing personal trauma or crisis situations expecting clinical support
  • Relying on AI emotional support as a primary mental health resource
