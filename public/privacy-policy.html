01

Who We Are

SomaSyncAI ("we," "us," or "our") is a clinical AI software platform operated by SomaSyncAI, a California-based technology company. Our platform, accessible at somasyncai.com, provides AI-assisted clinical documentation tools — including live in-ear guidance, voice-to-SOAP note generation, and treatment memory — for licensed manual therapy practitioners.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, protect, and share information when you use our platform. By using SomaSyncAI, you agree to the practices described here.

02

Information We Collect

We collect the following categories of information:

  • Account Information: Your name, email address, professional credentials (license number, discipline), and practice information when you create an account.
  • Voice & Audio Data: Audio captured during clinical sessions when you activate the voice charting or live AI guidance features. See Section 5 for full details.
  • Clinical Session Data: SOAP notes, session transcripts, patient encounter summaries, ICD-10 codes generated, and treatment memory records you create using the platform.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with the platform — features used, session duration, clicks, and navigation patterns.
  • Device & Technical Data: IP address, browser type, operating system, and device identifiers for security and debugging purposes.
  • Communications: Emails, feedback, and support messages you send us.

Important: SomaSyncAI is a practitioner-facing tool. We do not collect or store patient identifying information directly. Practitioners are responsible for ensuring their use of SomaSyncAI complies with applicable law regarding patient data, including obtaining proper consent before using voice features during clinical sessions.

03

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, operate, and improve the SomaSyncAI platform and AALIYAH.IO intelligence layer
  • Generate SOAP notes, treatment summaries, and ICD-10 code suggestions from your voice input
  • Build and improve treatment memory and clinical pattern recognition for your individual practice
  • Send you platform updates, security alerts, and account communications
  • Respond to support requests and feedback
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Detect and prevent fraud, abuse, and security incidents
  • Conduct aggregate, anonymized analysis to improve AI model accuracy — never using identifiable patient data

We do not use your data to train general-purpose AI models, sell to third parties, or serve advertising.

04

Clinical Data & HIPAA

SomaSyncAI is designed for use by licensed healthcare practitioners who are Covered Entities or Business Associates under HIPAA. The following applies to clinical data:

  • Practitioner Responsibility: You, as the licensed practitioner and Covered Entity, are responsible for ensuring your use of SomaSyncAI complies with HIPAA and applicable state privacy laws.
  • Business Associate Agreement (BAA): Practitioners who require a BAA for their practice should contact us at privacy@somasyncai.com. BAA availability varies by subscription tier.
  • Minimum Necessary: We apply the minimum necessary standard — only accessing clinical data required to provide the specific feature you're using.
  • De-identification: Any clinical data used to improve AI accuracy is de-identified in accordance with HIPAA's Safe Harbor or Expert Determination methods before use.
  • No Direct Patient Access: We do not interface directly with patients. All clinical data flows through you, the practitioner.

SomaSyncAI is a practitioner tool, not a covered healthcare provider. Patients do not create accounts on our platform. Their clinical information is captured through your voice and documentation — you remain the custodian of that information.

05

Voice & Audio Processing

SomaSyncAI's core features rely on voice input. Here is exactly how we handle audio:

  • Consent Required First: Our platform requires documented client consent before any voice features are activated. This is enforced at the application level — AALIYAH will not begin listening without a consent confirmation step.
  • Processing: Voice audio is processed in real time to generate text transcripts and clinical outputs. Audio is transmitted over encrypted connections (TLS 1.2+).
  • Retention: Raw audio files are not permanently stored by default. Transcripts and SOAP note outputs are retained in your account. You can delete these at any time.
  • Third-Party Processing: Voice-to-text processing may involve third-party AI services operating under strict data processing agreements that prohibit data retention and reuse.
  • Voice Calibration Data: Short calibration phrases you speak to set up AALIYAH are used solely for voice recognition accuracy within your session and are not stored permanently.
06

Data Storage & Security

We take the security of clinical data seriously and implement the following safeguards:

  • Encryption at Rest: All stored data is encrypted using AES-256 encryption.
  • Encryption in Transit: All data transmitted between your device and our servers is encrypted using TLS 1.2 or higher.
  • Access Controls: Access to clinical data is restricted to authenticated users. We use role-based access controls and multi-factor authentication for internal systems.
  • Infrastructure: SomaSyncAI is built on Supabase infrastructure, which provides enterprise-grade security, automated backups, and SOC 2 compliant data handling.
  • Breach Response: In the event of a data breach involving protected health information, we will notify affected practitioners within 60 days as required by HIPAA's Breach Notification Rule.

No system is 100% secure. We encourage you to use strong passwords, enable any available two-factor authentication, and log out of the platform when using shared devices.

Data Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information in the following limited circumstances:

  • Service Providers: We share data with trusted third-party vendors (cloud hosting, voice processing, analytics) who process data on our behalf under strict data processing agreements.
  • Legal Compliance: We may disclose information when required by law, court order, or governmental authority, or to protect the rights, safety, or property of SomaSyncAI, our users, or the public.
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your data may be transferred. We will notify you of any such change and your rights in connection with it.
  • With Your Consent: We may share information for other purposes with your explicit consent.

We never share clinical session data, patient encounter records, or SOAP notes with third parties for marketing, advertising, or commercial purposes.

Your Rights & Choices

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal information we hold about you.
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and associated data. Clinical session data will be deleted within 30 days of a verified deletion request.
  • Data Portability: Request an export of your data in a machine-readable format.
  • Opt-Out: Opt out of non-essential communications at any time via account settings or by emailing privacy@somasyncai.com.
  • Do Not Sell: We do not sell personal information. California residents may still submit a Do Not Sell request at privacy@somasyncai.com.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@somasyncai.com. We will respond within 45 days.
