The Full Session Workflow

A SESSION WITH AALIYAH

AALIYAH.IO is the AI intelligence powering SomaSyncAI. Here's exactly how a session flows from intake to documentation — step by step.

01
Client Arrives

Intake Form & Health History

Client fills out the intake form while the therapist prepares. No rushing, no skipping — a complete health history is the foundation of everything that follows.

Intake & Preparation
Calibration & Consent
02
Power On & Calibrate

AALIYAH Wakes Up

Therapist powers on the earpiece and opens the SOAP note dashboard. AALIYAH displays consent first — always. Then she runs a brief voice calibration sequence so she can recognize your voice precisely in any environment.

03
Health History Review

AALIYAH Analyzes for Contraindications

Therapist verbally reflects the client's intake form and health history out loud. As you speak, AALIYAH begins clinical analysis — checking for contraindications first, so no time is wasted. She may flag conditions requiring rescheduling, suggest orthopedic tests, or highlight specific symptoms to investigate. The more you reflect and listen with your client, the better the eventual treatment plan.

Clinical Analysis
Visual & Postural Assessment
04
Visual Assessment

Circle the Client. Call Out What You See.

Begin visual assessment — analyze gait, posture, and alignment while circling the client. Palpate problem areas. Call out what you see and feel out loud. AALIYAH captures every observation, merging Western anatomical analysis with TCM principles to build a complete clinical picture in real time.

05
Before Bodywork Begins

Generate Documentation — Review & Verify

Before beginning the bodywork session, hit Generate Documentation. AALIYAH produces a recommended treatment plan and full SOAP note draft from everything captured so far. The therapist double-checks, verifies, and approves before hands go on the client. This is where AI assists and the clinician decides.
